Massage Modalities

Services offered at Krave

Therapeutic Massage

Therapeutic massage combines several different massage modalities and techniques. The therapist will gauge your needs then apply the modality necessary for the most beneficial session using several strokes including swedish and myofascial techniques. Our goal with therapeutic massage is to be therapeutic and work out your muscle tension to relieve aches and pains, but with less pressure than a deep tissue session.  

CBD Massage

CBD Massage will bring you to your maximum relaxation level. CBD promotes faster healing and can naturally loosen muscles to reduce pain & inflammation. 
In session, we use a combination of our private label highest quality American Hemp salve and tincture.  CBD (or cannabidiol) contains NO THC and will not alter state of mind, it is strictly medicinal. 

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep tissue massage focuses on the deeper layers of thick muscle tissue to unstick the fibers of the muscles and release both toxins and deeply held tension points. Your therapist utilizes both trigger point therapy & sports massage techniques during session and you'll find that your body will continue to respond and release for a few days following treatment. 

Prenatal Massage

Carrying a baby changes your center of gravity and puts stress on your back, neck, abdominals, and shoulders. Pregnancy relaxes ligaments making pelvic joints less stable and changes posture.  Prenatal therapists know where to find the sore spots yet also know how to safely avoid areas which are contraindicated during pregnancy. We use a cushion designed for lying face down, yes, on your stomach!  We can also accomodate a side-line position if preferred.

Essential Oil Massage

Optimize your massage experience with essential oil which can increase relaxation and enhance your overall massage experience.  Essential Oils have been around as remedies for thousands of years. Your mind-body wellness will be enhanced with use of these amazing therapeutic oils.

Hot Stone Massage

Specially designed Hot Stones lathered in jojoba oil will be incorporated and used in intervals on and off during your session. Heat from the stones penetrates directly to the deeper layers of muscle tissue which can be extremely relaxing and effective in releasing tension while promoting a positive sense of comfort and well-being.

Craniosacral Therapy

CranioSacral therapy is a holistic healing practice that uses very light touching to balance the craniosacral system in the body.  The system includes the bones, nerves, fluids, and connective tissues of the cranium, the spinal area, and the sacrum - which are connected by a continuous membrane of connective tissue deep inside the body, called the dura mater.  Cerebral spinal fluid rises and falls within the compartment of the dura mater, known as the CranioSacral Rhythm (CSR).  CranioSacral therapist uses gentle touch to balance the flow of the CSR.  Once the cerebrospinal fluid moves freely, the body's natural healing response can function.  During a CranioSacral session, we use Lavender Essential Oil to enhance your relaxation and results.  

 

Foot Reflexology

Reflexology can be done on feet, hands, face & ears.  Our session is focused on the feet.   Foot Reflexology ​​​​​is a non-invasive way to bring your body in to balance through nerve endings and reflex points in the feet.  It can feel both stimulating and relaxing.  Hot Towels and Peppermint Aloe Foot Balm are used as an added bonus for this treatment. 

Lymphatic Drainage

The main function of the lymphatic system is detoxification.  A lymphatic session starts with resisted expansion of the rib cage, followed by circular motions on the neck, then pumping action on naval area where the biggest lymphatic vessels including the cisterna chyli are. We then move on to armpits, arms, legs and back focusing on lymph nodes and opening valves to allow accumulated lymph fluid to drain through proper channels. Dry Brushing and Grapefruit Essential Oil are incorporated into this session as a bonus in achieving optimal results. This session is designed with particular focus on the lymph system and is not considered your typical "massage".

Scalp Pampering

Add-on selection.  Adds 5-minutes to your session time.  "Top" off your massage!
It is our standard practice to touch on the scalp in our full body massages, but scalp pampering will ensure an additional 5-min of focused pressure point work on your occipitals, resulting in a 10-15 minute scalp massage during your session. Includes Scalp and occipital pressure point therapy, scratching, and gentle hair pulling.

 

Foot Pampering

Add-on selection.  Adds 5-minutes to your session time.  Add a little lovin' for your feet!
It is our standard practice to always work the feet in our full body massages, selecting foot pampering will ensure an additional 5-minutes focused "solely" on your feet, resulting in 10-15 minutes total on just your feet.  Includes Foot Pressure Point Massage, use of Hot Towels and soothing Peppermint-Aloe Foot Balm.

Mini-massage

Mini-massage is 30-minutes and is limited to choice of just one or two body parts for your therapist to focus on.  Or you can narrow it down by choosing "upper-body" or "lower-body".  In our regular 30-min session you can choose therapeutic or deep tissue.  
If you'd like to add CBD, you need to book a Mini-CBD session, but it will also include deep tissue if you'd like.
